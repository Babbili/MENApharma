Who We Are

We are a pharmaceutical consultation boutique, which is dedicated to help newly expanding companies into the Middle East, to better explore and understand these markets and finding suitable local distributors

We help you connecting and assessing potential local distributes, and suggest our recommendations

We can offer special case studies that focus upon specific niche therapeutic classes
Also, we offer sales and marketing pharmaceutical personnel hiring and training services

Mission & Vision

Vision statement, To become most trustable MENA regional pharmaceutical consultation firm for new market entrants

Mission statement, Providing professional and economic b2b pharmaceutical business consultations, for foreign companies which are newly exploring MENA markets, upon providing clear MENA markets' insights and suggesting best market entry strategy

Our services

Benefiting from our regional network allover MENA area, we can deliver in depth reports and access into MENA markets

Conducting market evaluation studies
Our studies will cover many points as general country and pharmaceutical market overview, registration process overview, pricing structure, average pharmaceutical consumption per capita, major international players, business sectors' overview, major provinces' contribution, number of physicians and pharmacies, competitors' prices for selected products and many other points
Specific niche market analysis can be done also

Linking and suggesting potential local distributors
We will offer you several suggested potential local distributors whom can help better market access, along with our analysis about their strength and weaknesses

Pharmaceutical sales and marketing personnel training services
Our courses include Professional Selling Skills, Pharmaceutical Brand Management, Distribution Area Management and many other courses
Our services are economic and of high professional standards
